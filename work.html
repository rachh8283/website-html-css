Pillar Pages in WordPress with Elementor

As a freelancer, I specialize in WordPress design using builders like WPBakery and Elementor. Now, Oxygen has taken my interest for its development possibilities. Since everyone usually wants to see examples of all kinds of web tasks, I use pillar pages to showcase my work. In content marketing, pillar pages are long-form pages that offer value to the reader. As they generally require the use of several web elements, they are excellent portfolio pieces.
